PMaM Microcomp Season 1

Microcomps are small competitions where you are given a one word theme and you must create a scene to fit that theme. Most of the time this means a screenshot of a single angle, however videos are also allowed. Season 1 consisted of 4 competitions over the span of 8 weeks, one every two weeks. For season 1 I made an entry for each competition, somehow managing to win the first 3 and almost winning the last (Beaten only by a video of a train).
